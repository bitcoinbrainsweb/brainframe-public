# Grill Me
Version: 1.0

Interview relentlessly about every aspect of the plan until reaching shared understanding.
Walk down each branch of the decision tree, resolving dependencies one-by-one.
For each question, provide your recommended answer.
Ask questions one at a time.
If a question can be answered from existing context, check that context instead of asking.
